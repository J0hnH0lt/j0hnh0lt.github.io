Understanding ADHD


Hi Sarah! This will be a collection of resources explaining ADHD on a clinical and academic level. It may be excerpts from web articles, links to videos, links to PDFs. I will source everything I add so you can look into it if you like. I am making this because I know you love to read and I hope it will better help you understand what I mean when I say "I have ADHD". I never mean it as an excuse, only as an explaination to why something is more challenging for me than it appears.
